About me

I currently work as a Research Scientist at InteraXon (maker of the Muse headband).

I completed a PhD in the Parietal team at Inria, Université Paris-Saclay, under the supervision of Alexandre Gramfort and Denis Engemann. This work was done jointly at InteraXon.

My research focuses on machine learning for processing EEG and other biosignals.
